Frequently Asked Questions

From March 2023, you will be able to access the online volunteers portal to submit your application. We will ask candidates to spend around 30 to 45 minutes filling out the online form, which will include questions relating to their identity, availability or experience for instance. To express their motivation, present their personality and explain what they hope to achieve from their mission, candidates will also be invited to answer questions whose answers will enable them to immediately check their suitability for each category of missions. They will also have the option to indicate the categories of missions for which they would not like to be offered a mission, but they will not be able to choose their mission. We would like to carefully review the applications and, for some missions, this will involve interviews with candidates. We will be able to inform candidates of our decision regarding their application during Automn 2023.

There will be 20,024 participants for the marathon format and 20,024 participants for the 10k format.

The Paris 2024 Olympic Games will take place from 26 July to 11 August.


The Paris 2024 Paralympic Games will take place from 28 August to 8 September.

You can now sign up to Le Club Paris 2024 to be kept up-to-date with ticket-related news and try to get priority access to ticket sales when they open.

We have chosen to organise the opening ceremony for the Paris 2024 Olympic Games at the heart of the City so that we can share this unique experience with even more people. At least 600,000 spectators will be able to be part of the celebrations, along the 6 km route, on the lower and upper quays of the River Seine. This is around 10 times the capacity of an Olympic stadium.
